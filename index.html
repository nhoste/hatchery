About

Expert Hatchery for Healthy Prawn Farming

We specialize in high-quality post-larvae prawn production, ensuring strong survival rates and disease-free stock. Our advanced hatchery techniques provide the best start for successful prawn farming.

With years of expertise, we focus on sustainable practices, maintaining optimal water conditions, and delivering premium-quality prawn larvae for farmers across the region.
